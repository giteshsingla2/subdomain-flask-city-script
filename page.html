Essential AC Maintenance Tips for Houston Homeowners

Living in Houston means your air conditioning system works overtime during our long, hot summers. Proper maintenance is crucial for keeping your AC running efficiently and avoiding costly breakdowns when you need cooling most. Here are expert tips from our experienced technicians:

Monthly Maintenance Tasks

Change your air filter every 30-60 days during peak summer months. Houston's humidity and occasional dust storms can clog filters quickly, forcing your system to work harder and use more energy. A clean filter improves air quality and system efficiency by up to 15%.

Seasonal Preparation

Before summer heat arrives, clear debris around your outdoor unit and ensure at least 2 feet of clearance on all sides. Trim vegetation and remove leaves, as blocked airflow can reduce efficiency by 10-25%. Check that your thermostat is working properly and consider upgrading to a programmable model to save energy when you're away.

Warning Signs to Watch For

Call professionals immediately if you notice warm air blowing from vents, unusual noises, frequent cycling on and off, or higher than normal energy bills. These often indicate refrigerant leaks, compressor issues, or electrical problems that require expert diagnosis.

Professional Maintenance Benefits

Annual professional tune-ups can prevent 95% of AC problems and extend your system's life by 5-10 years. Our comprehensive maintenance includes cleaning coils, checking refrigerant levels, testing electrical connections, and calibrating your thermostat. Most importantly, regular maintenance keeps your manufacturer's warranty valid.
