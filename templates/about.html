About Elite AC Repair

Your trusted partner for reliable air conditioning solutions since 2008. We're committed to keeping Houston comfortable, one home at a time.

Our Story

Founded in 2008 by Mike Rodriguez, Elite AC Repair began as a small family business with a simple mission: to provide honest, reliable air conditioning services to the Houston community. What started as a one-man operation has grown into a trusted team of certified technicians serving the greater Houston area.

With over 15 years of experience in the HVAC industry, we've built our reputation on quality workmanship, transparent pricing, and exceptional customer service. We understand that when your AC breaks down in the Houston heat, you need fast, reliable service you can trust.

Today, we're proud to be Houston's go-to AC repair specialists, serving thousands of satisfied customers across the metropolitan area. Our commitment to excellence and community involvement has made us a cornerstone of reliable home comfort solutions.
